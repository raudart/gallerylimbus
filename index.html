Information

Gallery Limbus is an online 3D gallery avaiable through the browser on your laptop or computer - no downloads or installations required. Press the two arrows in the bottom right corner to enter fullscreen and use Esc to exit. Use the W, A, S and D buttons to walk (or alternatively the arrow keys) and use your mouse to look around. Use shift to move faster. The experience uses sound, so be sure to use speakers or headphones before entering the gallery. Welcome to Gallery Limbus.

NOTE: If your cursor gets stuck while moving, use an actual mouse instead of a touchpad. If the gallery lags, use a stronger computer.

Find more art at: https://www.raudart.com/
